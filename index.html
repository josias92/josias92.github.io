About me

Hi, my name is Josias. I'm a design student at Eastern Washigton University in Cheney, WA, where I am pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree. My major is Visual Communications Design, with a minor in General Film Studies. I hope to do various projects that incorporate teachings from both fields.

My favorite design history movement would have to be Post Modern, I appreciate compositions that beg attention with a loud presence and require a tick more of time and effort to find meaning. In my designs though, I like simplicity and cleanliness so that information can be conveyed quickly without confusion.
